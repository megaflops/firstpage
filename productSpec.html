FAQ

How it works.

UV-C has long been proven as an effective technology to reduce contamination and the potential for infection by using short-wavelength ultraviolet (UV-C) light to kill or inactivate microorganisms by destroying nucleic acids and disrupting their DNA, leaving them unable to perform vital cellular functions. it effectively diffuses almost all kind of virus including COVID-19, bacteria and other harmful germs on any product surface.

Required Time for decontamination.

Decontamination process takes around 10 minutes.

Surfaces on which it works.

Product is designed and suitable for Surface like Plastic, Paper, glass, leather, metal, fabrics and many more.
